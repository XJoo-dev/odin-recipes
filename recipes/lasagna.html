Lasagna Roll-Ups

A thick and hearty lasagna roll-ups recipe filled with ricotta, Parmesan, and a beefy tomato sauce. A fun spin on a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ¼ white onion, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar chunky tomato sauce (such as Ragu®)
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 18 lasagna noodles
  • cooking spray
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 12 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 (12 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese, or as needed

Directions

  1. Cook and stir ground beef, onion, garlic powder, and oregano in a large skillet over medium heat until onion is tender and beef is browned, about 10 minutes.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbling, 30 to 35 minutes.

Burrito

In minutes, you can whip up this heart-smart snack.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup pinto beans
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 (10 inch) flour tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • ½ cup shredded lettuce
  • ½ tomato, diced
  • 2 tablespoons onion, chopped

Directions

  1. In a small dish, mash the beans with a fork; stir in salsa. Microwave tortilla on high for 15 seconds to soften.
  2. Spread bean mixture on tortilla and top with chopped vegetables.
  3. Fold up bottom and sides of tortilla and roll to make burrito.
  4. Microwave on high 1 minute. Top with sour cream.
